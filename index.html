About MonsterSurvivors

MonsterSurvivors is an action-packed roguelike survival game where you fight against endless waves of monsters. Each run is unique, with random enemy spawns and power-ups.

Game Highlights:

  • Procedurally generated levels ensuring no two games are the same
  • Dozens of weapons, passive items, and power-ups to discover
  • Beautiful pixel art graphics with smooth animations

MonsterSurvivors is easy to learn but difficult to master. How long can you survive?
